TRU-CRAFT (Computing Research Analytics For Teaching) is a research initiative focused on applying learning analytics and educational data science methods to improve teaching and learning outcomes.

Our research explores:
- Student engagement patterns
- Predictive modeling of student success
- Fairness and equity in educational models
- Cross-institutional transfer learning
